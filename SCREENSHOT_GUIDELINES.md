# Screenshot Specifications & Guidelines

## Overview
Screenshots are critical for App Store Optimization (ASO) and conversion. These guidelines ensure consistent, professional screenshots for both stores.

---

## iOS App Store Screenshots

### Required Sizes

#### iPhone (6.5" Display) - REQUIRED
- **Device:** iPhone 14 Pro Max, iPhone 13 Pro Max, iPhone 12 Pro Max
- **Resolution:** 1290 x 2796 pixels (portrait)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Color Space:** sRGB or P3
- **Quantity:** 1-10 screenshots

#### iPhone (5.5" Display) - REQUIRED
- **Device:** iPhone 8 Plus, iPhone 7 Plus, iPhone 6s Plus
- **Resolution:** 1242 x 2208 pixels (portrait)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Quantity:** 1-10 screenshots

#### iPad (12.9" 2nd/3rd Gen) - REQUIRED if supporting iPad
- **Resolution:** 2048 x 2732 pixels (portrait)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Quantity:** 1-10 screenshots

### Screenshot Strategy (Recommended Order)

1. **Hero Shot** - Main app view with key value proposition
   - Show "The List" rankings with attractive data
   - Include tagline: "Your Pool League, Organized"

2. **Challenge Feature** - Show challenge creation/management
   - Demonstrate how easy it is to challenge players
   - Highlight game type selection

3. **Match Tracking** - Match detail screen
   - Show completed match with scores
   - Display match history

4. **Treasury** - Financial management
   - Show treasury balance and transactions
   - Highlight transparency features

5. **Activity Feed** - Social proof
   - Show recent league activity
   - Demonstrate community engagement

### iOS Screenshot Best Practices

- **Status Bar:** Use clean status bar (9:41 AM, full battery, full signal)
- **Text:** Use large, readable text (captions on screenshots)
- **Localization:** Create separate screenshots for each supported language
- **No Transparency:** Screenshots must be opaque (no alpha channel)
- **No Masks:** Don't use device frames in actual uploads (App Store adds them)

## Google Play Store Screenshots

### Requirements
- **Resolution:** 320px to 3840px (any aspect ratio acceptable)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Max File Size:** 8MB per screenshot
- **Quantity:** 2-8 screenshots minimum (recommended: 5-8)

### Recommended Sizes

#### Phone Screenshots (REQUIRED)
- **Resolution:** 1080 x 1920 pixels (9:16 ratio)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 or 10:16
- **Quantity:** Minimum 4, maximum 8

#### 7-inch Tablet (OPTIONAL but recommended)
- **Resolution:** 1080 x 1920 pixels
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 or 10:16

#### 10-inch Tablet (OPTIONAL but recommended)
- **Resolution:** 1440 x 2560 pixels
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 or 10:16

### Feature Graphic (REQUIRED for Google Play)
- **Resolution:** 1024 x 500 pixels
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Purpose:** Displayed at top of store listing
- **Safe Zone:** Keep key content within 770 x 350 center area

### Google Play Screenshot Best Practices

- **Device Frames:** Optional but recommended for consistency
- **Captions:** Add brief captions explaining each feature
- **Localization:** Support all target languages
- **Content:** Show actual app UI (no mockups or renders)

## Screenshot Content Guidelines

### DO:
✅ Show actual app functionality
✅ Use realistic data (not "Test User" or "Lorem Ipsum")
✅ Maintain consistent branding (colors, fonts)
✅ Highlight unique features
✅ Show diverse users (if showing profile photos)
✅ Keep screenshots current with app version

### DON'T:
❌ Include device frames in actual upload files (iOS)
❌ Show competitor apps or brands
❌ Include pricing information (may vary by region)
❌ Use sexual, violent, or offensive content
❌ Show jailbroken/rooted devices
❌ Include hardware buttons in screenshots

## Preview Video (Optional but Recommended)

### iOS App Preview
- **Length:** 15-30 seconds
- **Resolution:** Same as screenshots
- **Format:** H.264 or ProRes 422
- **Audio:** Optional (most previews are silent)
- **Content:** Show 3-5 key features in sequence

### Google Play Promo Video
- **Length:** 30 seconds to 2 minutes
- **Resolution:** 1920 x 1080 (landscape)
- **Format:** YouTube video URL
- **Content:** Feature overview with voiceover optional
